* v0.9.0 d758807 -- 2022-12-22
  * Update `tools.build` to v0.9.0 (and remove the workaround added to `build-clj` in v0.8.5 since [TBUILD-34](https://clojure.atlassian.net/browse/TBUILD-34) has been fixed).
